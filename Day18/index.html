Halloween

Here are some interesting facts about halloween:

1. The holiday dates back more than 2,000 years
2. Trick-or-treating has existed since medieval times
3.The Irish also brought us jack-o'-lanterns
4. They used to be carved out of turnips, potatoes and beets
5. There's also traditional Halloween bread in Ireland
6. Candy corn was originally called "chicken feed"
7. You can even visit a pumpkin patch in Hawaii
8. The fastest pumpkin carving ever only took 16.47 seconds
9. New York City throws the biggest Halloween parade in the world.
10. Some shelters used to suspend black cat adoptions for Halloween
11. The night before Halloween is called Mischief Night or Goosey Night
