Social Services

Definition: Social services refer to a range of public and private services designed to support and enhance the quality of life for individuals and communities. These services are often provided by government agencies, non-profit organizations, or other entities with a focus on addressing social issues and meeting the basic needs of the population.

Personal Services

Definition: Personal services are services provided to individuals to meet their personal needs or enhance their well-being. These services are often tailored to the specific requirements of an individual and may include assistance with daily activities, personal care, and other services that contribute to an individual's overall comfort and satisfaction.

Business Services

Definition: Business services encompass a wide range of activities and support functions that are essential for the operation and growth of businesses. These services are often provided by specialized companies to help businesses streamline their operations, manage resources effectively, and achieve their goals.
